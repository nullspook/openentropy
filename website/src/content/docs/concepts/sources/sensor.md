---
title: 'Sensor Sources'
description: 'Entropy from analog sensor channels and ambient RF/noise environments'
---

Sensor sources expose analog-domain variability from audio, imaging, RF, and
device telemetry paths.

## Sources

- `audio_noise` — microphone ADC noise
- `camera_noise` — sensor dark/read noise
- `bluetooth_noise` — BLE ambient RF variability
- `smc_highvar_timing` — SMC sensor/timing channel variation
