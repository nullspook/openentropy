---
title: 'Thermal Sources'
description: 'Oscillator drift and thermal-noise influenced timing sources'
---

Thermal sources capture jitter induced by temperature-sensitive oscillators and
clock domains.

## Sources

- `audio_pll_timing` — audio PLL drift/jitter
- `counter_beat` — beat-frequency between independent clocks
- `display_pll` — display PLL phase-noise effects
- `pcie_pll` — PCIe clock-domain jitter
